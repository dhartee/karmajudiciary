About Us

Karma Judicary is a coaching institute in Indore that specializes in preparing students for judicial exams, including Civil Judge, ADPO, and other related law examinations.

Key features

Courses offered: Aakar Law offers various courses for judicial exams, including specific courses on new criminal laws (BNS, BNSS, BSA) and the MP Civil Judge full course.

Online and offline classes:The institute provides both online live classes and offline courses for students.

Digital platform:Aakar Law has a dedicated mobile app where students can access interactive classes, study materials, notes, and online tests..

Study materials: The institute provides course materials and notes, which are regularly updated for students.
